About Patna

Patna, the capital city of Bihar, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. It is situated on the southern bank of the Ganges River.

Patna is known for landmarks such as Golghar, Patna Sahib Gurudwara, and the iconic Gandhi Maidan. The city has played a significant role in Indian history, being a prominent center during the Mauryan Empire.

Today, Patna stands as a hub of education, culture, and tourism, attracting visitors from all over the world.
